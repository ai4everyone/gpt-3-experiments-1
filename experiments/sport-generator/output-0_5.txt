
Sport: Capture the Flag
Setup: Two teams of 15 players each face off in a large field with two flags in play, trying to capture the other team's flag and bring it back to their own flag. Points are scored when a team has captured the other team's flag.
Points: Each flag captured is worth 1 point.
Win condition: First team to 5 points wins.

Sport: Handball
Setup: Players are assigned numbers, and line up on opposite sides of a field. Players attempt to throw a ball into the opposing team's goal.
Points: Players are awarded one point for each goal scored.
Win condition: First team to 5 points wins.

Sport: Dumpball
Setup: Two teams of ten players each are placed in a large field with a large ball. The object of the game is to throw the ball into the opposing team's "dump", which is a large hole dug in the ground. Points are scored when a team's ball is closest to the dump.
Points: Each shot earns one point for the closest team to the dump.
Win condition: First team to 5 points wins.

Sport: Cloud Climbing
Setup: Two teams of at least three players each are placed on opposite sides of a cloud. The teams must climb to the top of the cloud without falling off. The team that reaches the top first wins.
Points: The team that reaches the top first wins.
Win condition: First team to reach the top of the cloud wins.

Sport: Slo-Pitch
Setup: A game of slo-pitch is played on a field with a small, hard ball and a long, thin bat.
Points: Players attempt to hit the ball as far as possible.
Win condition: The team that hits the ball the farthest wins.

Sport: Egg Football
Setup: Two teams of five players each are given an egg. They must attempt to carry the egg across the field to the other team's end zone, while preventing the other team from doing the same.
Points: When a player is tagged with an egg, that team loses one point.
Win condition: First team to five points wins.

Sport: Lightning
Setup: A "lightning bolt" (a long piece of wood) is placed in the center of a circle drawn in the dirt. Teams take turns throwing a tennis ball at the lightning bolt.
Points: If a player's ball lands closest to the lightning bolt, that player's team earns one point.
Win condition: First team to 5 points wins.

Sport: Plinko
Setup: Two teams of three players each sit on opposite sides of a Plinko board. The Plinko board is constructed with two long boards with a wide, shallow trough in the middle. Players are given a small amount of coins to start the game, and take turns dropping their coins into the board.
Points: Coins that land in the trough earn points for the team. Coins that land in the small hole at the end of the board earn bonus points.
Win condition: First team to 10 points wins.

Sport: Balloon Darts
Setup: Players use darts to pop balloons on the other team's side of the field. Points are scored when a player's dart lands closest to the center of a balloon.
Points: Each shot earns one point for the closest team to the center of the balloon.
Win condition: First team to 5 points wins.

Sport: Speedquidditch
Setup: The game is played on a large field, with a goal at each end. A large ball is placed in the center of the field, and players from each team try to keep the ball in play as long as possible.
Points: When a ball touches the ground, the last team to touch it gains 1 point. If a ball goes out of bounds, the last team to touch it loses 1 point.
Win condition: First team to 21 points wins.
