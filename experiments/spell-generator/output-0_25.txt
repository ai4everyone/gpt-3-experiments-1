Spell: Healing
Element: Water
Incantation: "Mwasha-shu swoshi-su"
Description: The caster must chant the incantation and then place their hands over the wound of the person they wish to heal. The wound will begin to glow with a blue light, and the wound will slowly close and heal.

Spell: Fireball
Element: Fire
Incantation: "Alla-hu fire-jon buu"
Description: The caster makes a fist and holds it tightly with their other hand while chanting the incantation. Their fist will glow red-hot until it combusts into a ball of flame, which can then be "punched" to launch a single fireball. The fireball will explode when making contact with a solid object.

Spell: Mind Reading
Element: Cosmic
Incantation: "Ish-ta-nim"
Description: The caster must concentrate on the target's face and repeat the incantation. While doing so, the caster's eyes will glow blue and they will be able to see the target's thoughts.

Spell: Telekinesis
Element: Cosmic
Incantation: "Dwam-mwah-shu"
Description: The caster must concentrate on the object they wish to move and chant the incantation. The caster can move the object with their mind, but they must be able to see the object to do so.
