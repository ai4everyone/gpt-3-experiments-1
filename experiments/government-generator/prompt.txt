The government of Phyrr is an authoritarian dictatorship based on a total theocracy under the Arbiter. All disputes, including those within the official legal system, are settled by the Arbiter. The government is violent in their suppression of any trace of opposition.

The government of Mialgonn is a scientific meritocracy based on technological and scientific progress. Historically, this government was a representative democracy until the assassination of a popular president forced the restoration of scentific rule.

The government of Magnarr is a stratocratic oligarchy based on the superior virtue of those who are considered "male". The government is expansionist and views all others as suspect.

The government of Vagh is a confederacy based on regional and ethnic affiliations. It claims authority over the region, although the other governments do not recognize that claim.

The government of Jurlin is a representative democracy based on equality. It is religiously neutral and promotes freedom of worship, including atheism, as well as cultural diversity. It is extremely welcoming of foreign citizens and values their contributions to society.
